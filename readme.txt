# Snake Game
Open with live server.
Snake Game is a classic arcade game where you control a snake that grows in length as it eats food. The objective is to eat as much food as possible without colliding with the walls or yourself.

*Note:** This version of the Snake Game is designed for desktop or laptop use and may not work optimally on mobile devices. For the best gaming experience, we recommend playing on a computer with a keyboard.

## Gameplay

- Use the arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to control the snake's direction.
- The snake will move continuously in the chosen direction.
- Try to eat the red food squares to increase your score.
- Be careful not to run into the walls or collide with your snake's own body.
- The game ends when the snake collides with a wall or itself.

## Features

- Simple and addictive gameplay.
- Score tracking to keep a record of your performance.
- Highscore display to challenge yourself to beat your best score.

## How to Play

1. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the snake.
2. Eat the red food squares to increase your score.
3. Try to achieve the highest score without colliding with the walls or yourself.
4. Your highscore will be saved locally in your browser.
